Submit SunVox Song

Submit a song to the SunVox Music YouTube channel!
To submit a song, please do one of the following:
  1. Email me (stereofield2015@gmail.com)
  2. Talk to me on the #submissions channel of the Sunvox Slack Channel.
Make sure you provide your song title and artist name as they will appear on the video, as well as your website(s) if you wish. I'll reply to your email/message ASAP.
Please note that your song *may* be rejected if it doesn't pass the screening process.
If your song gets rejected:
Don't take it personally. The idea that for every one good drawing, you produce a hundred bad ones applies to music as well. Making songs that aren't perfect is part of music. Keep going, and try hard! Music is great and fun, especially with SunVox! ;)
